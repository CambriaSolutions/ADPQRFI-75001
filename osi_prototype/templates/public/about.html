About

This prototype is developed by Cambria Solutions for the Office of Systems Integration (OSI) on behalf of the California Health and Human Services (CHHS) Agency using human centered design principles, agile development methodology, and open source technologies. We are building a tool that will empower foster parents and caseworkers to find and provide safe, temporary homes for kids who cannot remain safely in the home of their parent(s). Our goals are to get the best temporary placement for kids, link foster parents to resources and support, and build a true partnership between foster parents and their caseworker that ultimately addresses the education, mental and physical well-being of the children.

Cambria Solutions is a Sacramento based information technology and management consulting firm. We build custom-tailored solutions using ingenuity and a distinctly humanized approach.
